EVEN WORSE, PENGUINS COULD ALSO BECOME ENDANGERED DUE TO THE IMPACT OF POLLUTION.

Here's how trash can impact penguins:

  • I. Ingestion of Plastic: Penguins often mistake plastic for food, leading to injuries or death.
  • II. Habitat Pollution: Oil spills and trash pollute penguin habitats, especially affecting breeding grounds.
  • III. Entanglement: Penguins can get caught in fishing nets or debris, leading to injury or drowning.
  • IV. Food Source Depletion: Pollution harms marine life like fish and krill, which penguins depend on for food.
